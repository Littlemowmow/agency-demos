What we treat

Care for the conditions that keep you off your feet.

From everyday aches to complex foot and ankle problems, we diagnose clearly and treat with a plan built around your life, not a one-size script.

Heel & arch pain

Plantar fasciitis, heel spurs, and Achilles trouble, eased with targeted, evidence-based treatment that gets to the cause.

Diabetic foot care

Regular monitoring, wound care, and prevention to protect the feet of patients with diabetes and lower the risk of serious complications.

Bunions & hammertoes

Conservative and surgical options for structural changes in the toes, matched to your pain level, your lifestyle, and your goals.

Ingrown & fungal nails

Gentle, prompt treatment for ingrown toenails and nail fungus, often resolved in a single visit with lasting results.

Sports injuries

Ankle sprains, stress fractures, and tendinitis. We get athletes and active adults moving again with the right recovery plan.

Custom orthotics

Precision-fitted insoles that redistribute pressure and correct alignment, built to give lasting relief across many conditions.

About the clinic

Trusted foot and ankle care, right here in Chelsea.

At Kleanthous Family Foot Clinic, we believe healthy feet change everything. When you move without pain, you stay active and live better. That is the simple idea behind every appointment we schedule on West Middle Street.

Podiatry should never feel intimidating. Whether you are a retiree managing long-term heel pain, a runner with a nagging stress fracture, or a parent watching how your child walks, you get the same plain talk, unhurried attention, and careful clinical judgment at every visit.

We are proud to serve Chelsea, Dexter, Manchester, Saline, and the wider Washtenaw County community. Most major insurance plans are accepted, and we hold time for same-week appointments so relief does not have to wait.
