What We Treat

Conditions & Services

From everyday discomfort to complex foot and ankle conditions — our clinic provides comprehensive podiatric care for patients of all ages.

Heel & Arch Pain

Plantar fasciitis, heel spurs, and Achilles tendon issues addressed with targeted, evidence-based treatment plans.

Diabetic Foot Care

Routine monitoring, wound care, and preventive strategies to protect the feet of patients with diabetes — reducing risk of serious complications.

Bunions & Hammertoes

Conservative and surgical options for structural deformities of the toes — personalized to your pain level, lifestyle, and goals.

Ingrown & Fungal Nails

Prompt, gentle treatment for ingrown toenails and nail fungus — often resolved in a single visit with lasting results.

Sports Injuries

Ankle sprains, stress fractures, tendinitis — we get athletes and active adults back in motion with the right recovery plan.

Custom Orthotics

Precision-fitted custom insoles that redistribute pressure, correct alignment, and deliver long-term relief for a wide range of conditions.

About the Clinic

Trusted foot & ankle care, right here in Chelsea.

At Kleanthous Family Foot Clinic, we believe that healthy feet change everything. When you're free from pain, you move more, stay active, and live better. That's the straightforward philosophy behind every appointment we schedule on West Middle Street.

Our clinic is built on the conviction that podiatry shouldn't feel intimidating. Whether you're a retiree managing long-term heel pain, a local runner dealing with a nagging stress fracture, or a parent concerned about your child's gait — you'll receive the same clear communication, unhurried attention, and expert clinical judgment at every visit.

We're proud to serve Chelsea, Dexter, Manchester, Saline, and the broader Washtenaw County community. Most major insurance plans are accepted, and we work hard to offer same-week appointments so you're not waiting for relief.
