What we offer

Complete care under one roof

From routine checkups to same-day emergencies, our team is equipped to keep your whole family smiling — comfortably.

New patient exams & cleanings

A thorough welcome exam, digital X-rays if needed, and a gentle professional clean — the foundation of a lifetime of healthy teeth.

Fillings & crowns

Tooth-coloured composite fillings and durable porcelain crowns that blend seamlessly — restoring strength without sacrificing appearance.

Teeth whitening

Professional-grade whitening — in-office or custom take-home trays — for results that store kits simply cannot match.

Emergency dental care

Sudden toothache, cracked tooth, lost filling? We reserve time daily for urgent cases. Call us — we will get you seen fast.

Kids' dentistry

Gentle, patient-paced visits designed to make little ones comfortable with the dentist from the very start — healthy habits that last a lifetime.

Why Jativa

Dentistry without
the dread

Jativa Family Dental was built on a simple belief: that visiting the dentist should never feel like something to put off. Our team combines modern clinical skill with a genuinely warm, unhurried approach — so every patient, from the anxious adult to the curious six-year-old, leaves feeling cared for, not just treated.

We are proud to serve Clinton and the surrounding Lenawee County community as a locally owned practice. That means decisions are made by the people in this office, for the people of this town — not a corporate schedule. When you call, a real person answers. When you need us fast, we find a way.

From preventive cleanings to cosmetic whitening to same-day emergency relief, everything your family needs is right here on West Michigan Avenue. We are always accepting new patients.
