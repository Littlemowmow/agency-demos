A Saline Summer Tradition

There's a moment every summer when the air smells like sunscreen and fresh-cut grass, and the only thing that makes sense is a soft-serve cone from Mickey's. We've been that moment for Saline families for years: a friendly walk-up window on West Michigan Ave where the ice cream is real, the smiles are genuine, and the line moves fast.

Mickey's Dairy Twist is family-owned and proud of it. Every day we open those windows, we're doing it fresh. Real soft-serve mix, not pre-made hard-pack, churned right here so the texture is exactly what a summer cone should be. From banana splits big enough to share to hand-spun malts that take you straight back to 1962, this is the real thing. Come find us.
