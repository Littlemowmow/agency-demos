Our story

Not just a coffee shop.
Chelsea's living room.

Zou Zou's Café has been a fixture of downtown Chelsea since we opened our doors on North Main Street. We're the place where neighbors catch up, remote workers find their desk, and anyone who needs a moment of warmth gets one — no strings attached.

Every cup we pour comes from beans we believe in, roasted close to home. Every pastry in that case was shaped by hand this morning. We're not a chain pretending to be cozy — we are cozy, and that's not something you can franchise.

When you walk through our door, you're not just a customer. You're a regular in the making.

Locally owned in downtown Chelsea Independent, not a franchise. Every dollar you spend stays in our community.
Local art on the walls, always Rotating shows featuring Chelsea-area artists. A café and a gallery — in one place.
Fresh-baked every single day Pastries are made before dawn. If you're here before 8 am, you might catch them warm out of the oven.
The kind of place you find only in small towns — and then you never stop going back.
