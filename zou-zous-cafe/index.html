Our story

Not just a coffee shop. Chelsea's living room.

Zou Zou's Cafe has been a fixture of downtown Chelsea since we opened on North Main Street. We're the place where neighbors catch up, remote workers find their desk, and anyone who needs a moment of warmth gets one, no strings attached.

Every cup comes from beans we believe in, roasted close to home. Every pastry was shaped by hand this morning. We're not a chain pretending to be cozy. We are cozy, and that's not something you can franchise.

When you walk through our door, you're not just a customer. You're a regular in the making.

  • Locally owned in downtown Chelsea Independent, not a franchise. Every dollar stays in our community.
  • Local art on the walls, always Rotating shows featuring Chelsea-area artists. A cafe and a gallery in one place.
  • Fresh-baked every single day Pastries are made before dawn. Come before 8 am, you might catch them warm from the oven.
