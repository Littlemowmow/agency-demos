Clinton's boutique
salon since 2010.

Anne's Cuts & More has been a fixture in Clinton, Michigan for over a decade — not because we're the biggest salon in the county, but because we're intentionally not. We keep the schedule small and the attention high, so every client who sits in our chair gets a stylist who's fully present.

Our team takes the time to understand your hair, your habits, and what you're actually going for — not just what's trending. Whether you're coming in for a shape-up or a full color transformation, you'll leave feeling like yourself, only sharper.
